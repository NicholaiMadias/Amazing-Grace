Testimony

A New Beginning

Voice of Jesus Ministry — Tampa, FL

Housing instability can strip away more than a roof. It takes your sense of safety, your ability to plan, and your hope for the future. For many in our community, Amazing Grace Home Living has been the bridge between uncertainty and a stable foundation.

"When I walked through the door, I didn't just find a room — I found people who believed in me when I had stopped believing in myself."

Our all-inclusive housing model removes the anxiety of juggling utility bills, internet costs, and unexpected expenses. Residents can focus on rebuilding their lives — finding work, reconnecting with family, and growing in faith.

Community

Strength in Community

Voice of Jesus Ministry — Largo, FL

The Seven Stars Arcade was born from a simple idea: that learning can feel like play, and play can carry eternal truth. Residents and neighbors gather around shared screens, working through biblical quizzes and gem-matching challenges that quietly teach virtues like empathy, justice, and wisdom.

"I didn't expect a game to make me think about how I treat people. But here I am, thinking about it every day."

Community is built in the small moments — sitting together, laughing together, encouraging one another to try again after a wrong answer. That is the spirit of this ministry.

Mission

The Open Door

Voice of Jesus Ministry — Tampa Bay Region

Jesus spoke to the church in Philadelphia: "I have placed before you an open door that no one can shut." This verse anchors our mission. We believe every person deserves an open door — to safe housing, to community, to the knowledge that they are loved.

"Amazing Grace didn't just give me a place to stay. They gave me back my dignity."

From Tampa to Largo, we continue to expand our ministry — guided by the belief that stability is not a luxury, but a foundation for transformation.
